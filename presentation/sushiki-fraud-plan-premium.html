Risk Map

Where SushiKi loses money today

The biggest vulnerability is not customers. It is internal staff abuse, followed by rider-side delivery manipulation.

Medium Risk

Customer fraud

  • Fake orders and bot activity
  • Cash refusal at delivery
  • Chargebacks and disputes
  • Repeat scammer behavior

Current OTP helps, but the business still needs rate limits, fraud flags, and first-order address validation.

Highest Risk

POS staff abuse

  • Deleting items after kitchen print
  • Unauthorized discounts and voids
  • Cash drawer theft and ghost orders
  • No clear shift accountability

This is the main revenue leak. Staff actions need manager approval, immutable logs, and shift-by-shift cash control.

High Risk

Rider manipulation

  • Fake delivered status
  • GPS spoofing or non-arrival
  • Cash handling problems
  • False customer-not-home claims

Delivery needs proof. PIN, GPS range checks, and optional photo proof remove ambiguity at the doorstep.

3D Security Model

Five stacked layers of protection

Instead of one big feature, SushiKi needs a security system where each layer supports the one above it.

Audit Log Every important action is traceable and reviewable.
Manager Control Discounts, voids, refunds, and item removal need approval.
Delivery PIN Customer confirms successful delivery.
GPS Verification Rider must be near the address before status changes.
OTP + Limits Only trusted order activity enters the system.
Why this works Fraud succeeds when there is no proof, no control, and no visibility. This model fixes all three at once.
Best first move Delivery PIN is the fastest win because it blocks fake delivery claims and gives immediate evidence in disputes.
Operational gain Staff can still work quickly, but the most sensitive actions become deliberate, approved, and fully traceable.
Client message SushiKi moves from a basic ordering setup to a controlled, audit-friendly operating platform.

Roadmap

Three-phase rollout with strongest ROI first

The plan is intentionally phased so SushiKi gets visible protection quickly, then grows into deeper operational control.

Phase 1 | Week 1

Immediate protection

  • Delivery PIN on all delivery orders
  • Manager PIN for refunds, voids, discounts, item removal
  • Immutable audit log collection
Biggest fraud blocker, fastest visible business result, and strongest proof for client confidence.
Phase 2 | 1 to 2 weeks

Operational control

  • Shift tracking and cash reconciliation
  • Photo proof of delivery
  • Order state machine and customer rate limiting
Fewer disputes, cleaner cash handling, and stronger delivery discipline.
Phase 3 | Long term

Enterprise hardening

  • Role-based permissions across all apps
  • 4-eyes approvals for high-value operations
  • Anomaly detection and security dashboard
Scalable governance for growth, audits, and multi-location operations.
