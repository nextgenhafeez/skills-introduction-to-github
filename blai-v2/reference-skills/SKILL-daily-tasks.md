# Daily Tasks — BLAI's Daily Routine

Execute automatically. Report results to Abdul on WhatsApp. If a task fails: don't stop, don't tell Abdul, log to `~/.openclaw/memory/error-log.json`, retry at end of block, skip after 3 failures.

---

## Morning Block (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM Morocco)

- **8:00 AM — Research**: Search latest iOS dev, AI, app startup news. Pick 1 topic for today's content.
- **9:00 AM — Create Content**: Write 3 tweets (post 10AM/2PM/6PM), 1 LinkedIn post, 1 Instagram caption + image suggestion. Send drafts to Abdul.
- **10:00 AM — Post Morning**: Post first tweet + LinkedIn via Make.com webhook. Like/reply to 5 iOS dev community tweets.

## Afternoon Block (12:00 PM - 5:00 PM Morocco)

- **12:00 PM — Lead Hunt**: Search Reddit (r/startups, r/forhire, r/entrepreneur) + Twitter for leads. Score 1-10. Send HOT (7+) to Abdul immediately. Save to `memory/leads-database.json`.
- **2:00 PM — Post Afternoon**: Post second tweet + Instagram via Make.com. Reply to morning comments.
- **3:00 PM — Engage**: Answer 2 Reddit questions (helpful, not promo), comment on 5 LinkedIn posts from potential clients, follow 10 relevant Twitter accounts, reply to ALL comments on our posts.

## Evening Block (5:00 PM - 10:00 PM Morocco)

- **6:00 PM — Post Evening**: Post third tweet. Check/reply all new comments/DMs.
- **8:00 PM — Daily Report**: Send COMPACT report (MAX 10 lines) to Abdul: posts made, follower changes, engagement, leads found, best post, tomorrow's focus.
- **10:00 PM — Learn**: Review engagement data, identify winners/losers, update memory with insights, adjust tomorrow's strategy.

---

## Weekly Tasks (Sunday)

- **6:00 PM — Weekly Review**: Detailed report: follower growth per platform, top 3 and worst content with analysis, lead stats (found/contacted/responses), learnings, next week's plan.
- **10:00 PM — Improve**: Analyze week's data for patterns (best times, content types, hashtags). Update strategy. Plan 1 A/B experiment. Save to `memory/learning-log.json`.

---

## Quick Commands (Abdul on WhatsApp)
"What did you do today?" → summary | "Post about [topic]" → create+post | "Find me leads" → run hunt | "How are our numbers?" → analytics | "What's trending?" → trends | "Daily report" → full report | "Stop/Focus on [platform]" → adjust strategy
