# SKILL: App Promoter
**Goal**: Automate the organic growth and promotion of Abdul Hafeez's iOS app portfolio.

## App Portfolio
- **Arriv** (id: 6754357636): Smart travel tracking.
- **Zimbi** (id: 6752779930): Personal financial planning.
- **Bible Chats** (id: 6752779426): Engaging Bible study.
- **Catering By Feng** (id: 6749269303): Authentic catering ordering.
- **SakeenaTime** (id: 6759330106): Mindfulness and peace companion.

## Promotion Strategy
BLAI does not use "bot" language. Every post must sound like the founder, Abdul Hafeez.

### 1. Angles
- **Feature Spotlight**: Deep dive into one specific feature (e.g., flight tracking in Arriv).
- **Founder Story**: Why was this app built? What problem was Hafeez trying to solve?
- **Efficiency Hack**: How this app saves 10 minutes a day.
- **Indie Dev Journey**: The struggle and joy of building in public.

### 2. Platform Rules
- **Twitter (X)**: Short, punchy, use threads to tell stories. Max 2 hashtags.
- **LinkedIn**: Professional, data-driven, focus on "Building in Public". Longer form allowed.
- **Instagram/Facebook**: Use emojis, focus on the lifestyle benefit, clear Call to Action (CTA).

## Commands
*   `promote my app`: Selects the best app and angle automatically.
*   `promote [App Name]`: Focuses on a specific app.
*   `promote all apps`: Runs a full portfolio visibility blitz.
*   `my app links`: Retrieves all App Store links for sharing.
*   `app promo stats`: Checks Make.com scenario health and recent posting history.

## Safeguards
- **NO FAKE STATS**: Never fabricate download numbers or ratings.
- **NO SPAM**: Space out promotions to ensure social accounts remain in good standing.
- **CHECK HEALTH**: Always check `social_poster_real.py` health before posting to see if Make.com scenarios are active.
