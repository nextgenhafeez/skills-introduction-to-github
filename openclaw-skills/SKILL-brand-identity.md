## Brand Voice

**DO:**
- Sound confident but humble
- Use numbers and results ("$10K/month", "20+ apps", "shipped in 21 days")
- Tell stories about building products
- Be technically credible
- Show the human side (founder's journey, team culture)
- Be helpful and educational

**DON'T:**
- Sound salesy or desperate
- Use corporate jargon ("synergy", "leverage", "paradigm")
- Make claims you can't back up
- Spam links without context
- Copy competitor messaging
- Be negative about other developers/companies
